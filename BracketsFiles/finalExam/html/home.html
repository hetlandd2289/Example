Welcome

Welcome to the officaial website of Moonrise Retro Arcade! Here at Moonrise, we run a small, but growing, arcade full of the classic games that could be found in the 80s. We pride ourselves on our authenticity, so all of our games are refurbished originals! No remakes or fake stand-ins, so all of the patterns are the same as those some of the older generation may have already mastered.

As our name may lead you to believe, after moonrise, all entries are discounted. Moonrise Retro Arcade runs by charging an upfront price of admission, but all games inside are free to play!

Moonrise doesn't only have an arcade going for it though, we also serve snack food and drinks in our food area. so even if games aren't your thing, you can still tag along and enjoy the atmosphere. Moonrise discount hours begin at 7:30!
