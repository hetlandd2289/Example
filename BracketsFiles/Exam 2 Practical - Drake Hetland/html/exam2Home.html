Welcome

We here at Brooks welcome all golfers, new and returning, this season. We hope you choose to golf with us, as we have a lot to offer this year.

If you are just getting in to golfing, don't worry. We have a lax dresscode, and a golf shop if you are in need of clubs and golfballs. If you plan on golfing a lot, give our membership deals a look. Planning on playing all 27 holes? No worries! We have a pub located on the edge of the course open for all golfers.
